## Open items
- L0-SETTLE-01: The settlement and its crew rotation shall be sustained safely and indefinitely: keep-alive and rotation logistics, without claiming a self-sufficiency it has not reached.
- L1-ROT-01: Crew rotation shall be sustained: at every window, deployed return capacity (berths at 100 pax/ship, banked methalox ~1,200 t/ship, ~50 t consumables/ship) shall meet the reserve policy - full evacuation of the resident population achievable over <= 3 departure windows, rolling-cohort reserve otherwise; the return ground segment (pads x service stands) shall clear scheduled + reserve departures within the ~30-day departure window (pad/stand count a computed output); and no rotating cohort shall be stranded more than one synod past its window.
- L1-LS-05: Life support shall be sustained indefinitely without Earth return: O2 recovery >= 75% and whole-loop water >= 95%; each crew-critical function shall carry N+2 redundancy including >= 1 dissimilar path (common-cause coverage); spares shall be Poisson-sized to < 1% stockout per function per synod on demonstrated (not estimated) failure rates; and a standing consumables + critical-spares reserve of >= one missed synod shall be held.
- L1-PWR-05: Power shall be sustained at settlement scale: installed generation shall exceed total load with >= 15% reserve margin at every window; installed generation minus the single largest unit shall cover the crew-critical (keep-alive) load, with agricultural grow-lighting shed to a crop keep-alive floor before any crew-critical curtailment; and a funded reactor-replacement cadence (fission units are ~10-year replace assets) shall keep the rolling fleet balance positive, replacements pre-positioned >= 1 synod ahead of end-of-life.
- L1-MED-01: Resident health shall be sustained with on-site definitive care (NASA-STD-3001 Level of Care V equivalent: surgery, trauma, imaging, pharmacy trending to on-site production, dental, mental health) and NO reliance on Earth medical evacuation for acute conditions; a surface medical reserve of >= one synod shall be held separately from and shall not draw down the return-propellant bank; no single provider or facility loss shall remove a life-critical capability (>= 2 surgically-competent providers). Reproductive/obstetric capability shall not be established before the ~1,100-resident settlement phase and, when established, shall be a coordinated (managed, gated) capability - free/open reproduction is a post-CONOPS-2 future goal, gated on characterisation of 0.38 g development.
- L1-HAB-03: Habitats and infrastructure shall be sustained indefinitely: steady-state atmospheric leakage shall be bounded with a make-up-gas reserve of >= one synod plus one credible breach; each pressure shell shall be certified to factor-of-safety 4.0 on pressure and lifetime (JSC-67721) with the structural shell - not regolith overburden - bearing the pressure load and redundant seals so no single seal failure depressurizes; >= 2-3 m of in-situ regolith radiation cover shall be maintained (sized by radiation, decoupled from dead-weight containment depth); and a recurring maintenance + spares provision of >= 10%/yr of installed sustainment-system dry mass shall be manifested (Earth resupply and/or in-situ manufacture) at < 1%/yr capability-loss probability.
- L1-COM-02: Continuous base-Earth communications shall be maintained through solar conjunction via a Sun-Earth L4/L5 (or heliocentric-drift) relay bridge; the orbital constellation shall be refreshed/augmented on a <= 2-synod cadence before design-life lapse (the window-0 seed expires ~2036-2038); bandwidth shall scale with population; and the segment shall be loss-tolerant (no single-node dependency) since it also carries EDL telemetry and launch weather-watch for rotation launches.
- L1-GRAV-01: Crew partial-gravity health shall be sustained: adult 0.38 g deconditioning shall be countered by exercise + pharmacologic countermeasures (with an optional short-arm centrifuge reserve), and in-situ human reproduction shall be gated until development in 0.38 g is characterised (linked to L1-MED-01).
